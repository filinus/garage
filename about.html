About

I’m a Product Designer with a passion for crafting meaningful, human-centered experiences. I thrive at the intersection of design, strategy, and systems thinking—transforming user needs into impactful, scalable solutions.

I believe great design is both intuitive and intentional. Every decision I make is rooted in user insight, and I’m driven by the challenge of connecting design, product, and engineering to build cohesive, high-performing experiences.

I hold a master’s degree in economics and am always exploring ways to blend creativity with precision. When I'm not designing, you’ll find me sketching ideas, hiking new trails, or chasing sunsets on my bike.
